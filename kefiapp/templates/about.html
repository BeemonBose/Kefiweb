The Company

Kefi Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd is an End-to-End Solutions Provider for all your Software Development Services based in Technopark, Trivandrum. Kefi Tech was founded in the year 2016 and have offices in India and Canada. Our development and delivery center is located in Trivandrum, India. The software development team and app development team based in Technopark are a cost effective outsourcing hub to many international clients in US, Canada and Middle East. Kefi is also specialized in offshore delivery of testing services.

We are an End-to-End IT services provider for all your Information Technology requirements. Whilst you focus on growing your business through your core functions, we come in the picture to help you with all of your non-core functions, not only with respect to IT but also on back end support functions with our BPO services.

Kefi Tech Solutions believes that following are the four key factors in delivering successful solutions consistently …

  • Adopting right technology and continuous upgrades - Requires in-depth practical knowledge of the technology augmented with continuous learning of new developments.

  • Process and its benefits - Predictability and consistency is what you get. Software engineering processes have matured a lot in the last decade and evolving to be more in line with the needs of businesses. We adopt and follow the right processes which ensures consistent delivery of high-quality solutions.

  • Adopting right tool - Improved time to market and ensures success with quality. Every activity in software development cycle benefits immensely by adopting right tools. Kefi is proficient in using project management tool, DeVops tool, test automation tool etc.

  • Experience matters - Ensures reduced risks and addresses most of them before it arises. Bringing an experienced team for a task is our core strategy to deliver quality solutions.

Kefi is the right sized IT service partner which is focused on value-based services which is at par with any leading service organisation in this space. We give utmost importance and care to your intellectual properties, confidentiality of data and information, aligning ourselves with your goals and work as one team to ensure your success.
